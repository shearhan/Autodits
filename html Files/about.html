About Autodits

Our Autodits Tool empowers users with a comprehensive system assessment, including a Full Audit, targeted evaluation of specific Benchmark sections, and assistance in implementing effective remedies for any identified issues.

Our automated audit tool provides various functionalities:

- Full Audit: This feature runs all sections of the Benchmark (Ubuntu LTS 22.04 v1.0).

- Sections: This capability enables the execution of individual sections within the Benchmark, spanning from section 1 to 6. Moreover, upon initiating the audit, users will gain access to comprehensive insights and in-depth information pertaining to any sections that may have encountered failures during the execution.

- Remediation: Implement the suggested solution/remediation for the sections that have experienced failures during execution.
